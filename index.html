About Aleppo Construction

Aleppo Construction LTD was established in Syria in 1994 with a team of around 12 employees and a capital of 10 million Syrian pounds. We began by building small residential homes, villas, and chalets, along with offering interior cladding and decoration services.

Over the years, the company followed a clear vision, focusing on steady growth and professionalism. This approach led to greater success, allowing Aleppo Construction to take on larger projects across the region.

Today, nearly 25 years later, a new and independent branch has opened in Nigeria, managed directly by the original founders. With a solid understanding of local construction needs and opportunities, Aleppo Construction LTD was officially incorporated on September 7, 2020, with registration number RC 1707916.

Featured Project

Our inaugural project showcases our commitment to excellence and attention to detail in every aspect of construction.

The Nest Apartments

Situated at Plot 13, Kayode Animashaun Street, just off Admiralty Road in Lekki Phase 1, The Nest Apartments brings contemporary urban living to one of Lagos’ most desirable residential neighborhoods. The building offers both accessibility and privacy, positioned near the commercial pulse of Lekki while maintaining a calm, residential character.

This six-storey structure is thoughtfully crafted with a strong emphasis on spatial efficiency and modern aesthetics. Clean lines, bold geometry, and carefully selected materials give the development a distinct architectural presence. Each unit is designed to meet the needs of professionals, couples, and small families seeking a refined, low-maintenance lifestyle.
